Overview

A computer application and motion graphic based on the novel “Ready Player One” by Ernest Cline. In the novel there are many 80’s references. This project conglomerates them and combines them into a game-like program which can be viewed in the video.

Details

class: Digital Portfolio

duration: 4 weeks

date: January 2018

It was important to me that the icons stuck to the 80’s theme as well as best represented the novel itself.
